Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing?

Tap the "Sell" button on the main screen, add photos of your item, fill in the details (title, price, description, category), and tap "Post Listing".

How do I contact a seller?

Tap on any listing to view details, then tap the "Contact Seller" button to send them a message.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We use Firebase Authentication to securely protect your account. We never share your personal information with third parties.

How does location work?

We use your device's location to show you nearby listings. You can browse by map or filter by distance. Your exact location is never shared with other users.

What is Premium Boost?

Premium Boost highlights your listing at the top of search results for 7 days, helping you sell faster.

How do I delete my listing?

Go to your Profile, tap on the listing you want to delete, then tap the delete button.

Can I trade items instead of selling?

Yes! When creating a listing, toggle "Trade Only" and specify what you're looking for in exchange.
