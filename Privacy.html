Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Off Roader Up ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (optional)

1.2 Listing Information

When you create a listing, we collect:

  • Item photos and descriptions
  • Price and category information
  • Location information (city/region only, not exact address)

1.3 Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to:

  • Show you nearby listings
  • Display items on the map view
  • Calculate distances to items

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings. Your exact location is never shared with other users.

1.4 Camera and Photo Library

We request access to your camera and photo library only when you choose to:

  • Take photos for listings
  • Upload photos from your library
  • Update your profile picture

1.5 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Process and display your listings
  • Show relevant nearby items
  • Improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Send important notifications about your account
  • Respond to support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, Google's mobile platform. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data:

  • Publicly visible listings: Item photos, descriptions, location (city/region), and seller name are visible to all users
  • With service providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage
  • When required by law: To comply with legal obligations or protect rights and safety

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update information: Edit your profile and listings anytime
  • Delete your account: Contact us to permanently delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of location services: Disable location access in device settings
  • Control notifications: Manage notification preferences in app settings

6. Children's Privacy

Off Roader Up is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore: For secure login and data storage (Firebase Privacy)
  • Expo: Development platform (Expo Privacy)

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Public listings may remain visible until manually removed
  • Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics

9. International Users

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where Firebase operates. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@offroadup.com
  • Support: offroadup.com/support
